Digital confidence for adults who refuse to be left behind

Learn Linux, home networking, AI, and digital safety without fear.

REBUILD45 is a beginner-friendly learning lab for adults over 40 who want to understand modern technology without intimidation, gatekeeping, or unnecessary jargon.

The mission

Remove fear. Build familiarity.

This project is about learning the systems already running your life: your computer, your phone, your Wi-Fi, your data, and the modern internet.
